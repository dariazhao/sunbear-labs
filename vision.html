Theory of Change

From tools to transformation

We don't fix schools. We strengthen foundations.

Research meets practice

When every educator and caregiver has research-backed, AI-assisted tools, every child gets developmentally appropriate experiences, regardless of their program's budget or their teacher's training background.

  • Expert-level developmental knowledge, made accessible to everyone
  • Personalized activity recommendations at the individual child level
  • Readiness gaps close before they calcify into achievement gaps

Why AI, why now

Large language models can finally personalize at the individual child level without requiring a 1:1 adult ratio. AI can make expert developmental knowledge accessible to a family childcare provider in rural Mississippi or a grandmother raising grandchildren.

  • If we can't build AI that helps a 4-year-old learn, we have no business building AI at all
  • Early childhood is the proof case for responsible, beneficial AI
  • The intersection of scale and intimacy that only AI can solve

Where we're headed
