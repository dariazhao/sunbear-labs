Ecological Systems Model

Evidence-to-practice translation at scale.

Decades of research confirm that child outcomes are shaped by the full ecology of care: family, classroom, and community. Our platform embeds validated developmental frameworks directly into the tools these microsystems already use, closing the research-to-practice gap that leaves the most under-resourced communities without access to evidence-based programming.

Dual-Generation Intervention

The intersection of scale and intimacy.

Generative AI enables individualized developmental support at population scale without requiring prohibitive staffing ratios. Our dual-generation model simultaneously builds caregiver capacity and improves child outcomes, targeting the two variables that longitudinal research identifies as most predictive of kindergarten readiness.
