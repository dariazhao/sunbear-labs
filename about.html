Our Thesis

The readiness gap

Three decades of research point to the same conclusion: early childhood is the most under-resourced stage of education.

90%

Of brain development happens

Before age 5, over one million neural connections form per second — yet early childhood remains the most under-invested segment of education.

Harvard Center on the Developing Child
34%

Of 4-year-olds in public pre-K

Two out of three children lack access to publicly funded pre-K. The families who need the most get the least.

NIEER Yearbook
$7–13

Return on every $1 invested

Nobel laureate James Heckman proved early childhood yields the highest ROI of any public investment in human capital.

Heckman Equation, 2012

Sunbear Labs translates that evidence into free, practical resources for the people closest to kids.

Who We Serve

Built for everyone in the readiness ecosystem

Different audiences. One shared mission.

Tools that fit your real classroom constraints. Designed with Head Start teachers, family childcare providers, and pre-K educators. Save time on planning, track readiness with confidence, and access research-backed activities that actually work.
Free activities, milestone trackers, and bilingual resources that extend learning from the classroom into the home. See your child's progress, celebrate their growth, and know exactly how to support their readiness journey.
Early childhood is the highest-ROI investment in human capital. Sunbear Labs is building the infrastructure layer for a $50B+ market, with a free-core model that drives adoption and a platform that generates the outcome data funders need.
