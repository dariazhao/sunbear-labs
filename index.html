Your FAQ

From educators, families, and program operators exploring Sunbear Labs.

Sunbear Labs is an early childhood education platform built to help every child walk into kindergarten ready. We bring together activity tools, curriculum frameworks, progress reporting, and family engagement resources — all grounded in research and designed for the real-world constraints of pre-K and Early Learning programs.
Our core tools are always free. That includes readiness activity kits, family guides, and milestone tracking for individual children. Additional capabilities like cohort reporting, grant documentation, and advanced analytics are available for programs and networks at scale that need more.
We're starting in the San Francisco Bay Area, with community partners in Alameda, Marin, and San Mateo counties. Our tools are already being used by families across 38 states, and we're actively expanding our community partnership model. If you're a program operator or school district outside the Bay Area, we'd love to connect.
Sunbear Labs is built for three main audiences: pre-K educators and Head Start programs looking for evidence-based tools to build kindergarten readiness; families who want to actively support school readiness at home; and program operators managing childcare networks, cohort reporting, and grant documentation. If you work with children ages 2–6, Sunbear is built for you.
Yes. Our activity frameworks and curricula are aligned to state K-entry standards and Head Start Early Learning Outcomes Framework (ELOF). We regularly review and update our content to reflect the latest research in early childhood development and state-level requirements.
