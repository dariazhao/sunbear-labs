Where it started
A decade in the making.

In 2018, we left investment banking and biomedical engineering to build Learn With Mochi. A screen-free, story-driven coding kit loved by families in 60+ countries around the world.

What we learned
Stories are the curriculum.

Mochi taught us that when you wrap a big idea in a great story, children absorb it without trying. Play isn't the break from learning. It is the learning.

Families said it best
The proof was in the play.

"Mochi inspires growing brains and curious hands. My daughter asks for it by name every single day."A Mochi parent, Ohio

Earned your trust
The world took notice.

#1 on Product Hunt. Forbes' Top STEM Toy of 2024. Purdue Engineering's top pre-college gift pick. When products are built with trust, care, and safety, learning is inevitable.

60+ countries

Mochi shipped globally. Every box that arrived on a doorstep was proof: parents and educators everywhere want the same thing for their kids, and deserve real tools to get there.

Your FAQ

From educators, families, and program operators exploring what Sunbear Labs can do.

Sunbear Labs is an early childhood education playground built to help every child walk into kindergarten ready. We bring together assessment tools, curriculum frameworks, progress reporting, and family engagement resources. Our programs, tools, and resources are grounded in research and designed for the real-world constraints of Pre-K programs.
Our core tools are always free. That includes readiness assessments, family activity guides, and milestone tracking for individual children. Additional capabilities like cohort reporting, grant documentation, and advanced analytics are available for programs and networks at scale that need more.
We're starting in the San Francisco Bay Area, with community partners in Alameda, Marin, and San Mateo counties. Our tools are already being used by families across 38 states, and we're actively expanding our community partnership model. If you're a program operator or school district outside the Bay Area, we'd love to connect.
Sunbear Labs is built for three main audiences: Pre-K educators and Head Start programs looking for evidence-based assessment and curriculum tools; families who want to actively support school readiness at home; and program operators managing childcare networks, cohort reporting, and grant documentation. If you work with children ages 2–6, Sunbear is built for you.
Yes. Our assessments and curriculum frameworks are aligned to state K-entry standards and Head Start Early Learning Outcomes Framework (ELOF). We regularly review and update our content to reflect the latest research in early childhood development and state-level requirements.
